Who We Are

At Bonin Masonry, Inc we are focused on providing masonry and hardscape services with the highest levels of customer satisfaction – we will do everything we can to meet your expectations. With a variety of services to choose from, we’re sure you’ll be happy working with us. Look around our website and if you have any comments or questions, please feel free to contact us. We hope to see you again! Check back later for new updates to our website. There’s much more to come! Bonin Masonry, Inc has been owned and operated by Russell Bonin since 1987. We are experienced in residential and commercial masonry. In the residential field we specialize in chimneys, fireplaces, stone walls, patios and walkways. In the commercial field we have experience in block buildings and brick/stone veneers.

Russell Bonin – Owner
Russell was first introduced to masonry while he was still in high school. After college he went to work for a local mason contractor and later started his own business. He called it Russell Bonin Masonry. His first full year in business was in 1987. He started out small but quickly grew into a full service masonry company. Russell became well known for his expertise in stone work, fireplaces, chimneys, stone walls and patios. Russell is now the pastor of a church in Searsport, Maine and runs the masonry business with his son Dan.

Dan Bonin – Owner
Dan has been involved in masonry since he was old enough to help his dadon some of the smaller projects. Dan went to college at Bob Jones University in Greenville, SC and graduated in 2012 with a B.S. in General Construction Management. After graduating, he came back to Maine and has been running the business full time. Along with all of his responsibilities with the masonry business, Dan also is a licensed real estate agent in Maine.
